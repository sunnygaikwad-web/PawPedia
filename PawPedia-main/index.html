Pet Care

Your pet deserves the best.

🍖

Feeding

Adult dogs need 2 meals/day. Puppies 3–4. Always fresh water available.

🚿

Grooming

Brush coat 2–3x weekly. Bathe every 4–6 weeks. Trim nails monthly.

🏃

Exercise

At least 30–60 min of active play or walks per day for a healthy dog.

💉

Health

Annual vet checkup. Core vaccines: Rabies, Distemper, Parvo, Hepatitis.
